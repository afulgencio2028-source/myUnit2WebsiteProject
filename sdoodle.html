Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup room temperature butter
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspooms of cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • cinnamon sugar: 1/4 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Jim Winstead - Wikimedia Commons - snickerdoodles

Instructions

STEP 1: Beat the softend room temperature 3/4 cup of butter, 3/4 cup of brown sugar, and 1/4 cup white suagr until super light and fluffy.

STEP 2: Add 1 room temperature egg and 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture.

STEP 3: Then in a SEPARATE BOWL, whisk together all the dry ingredients - 2 cups of flour, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1 1/2 teaspoons of cinnamon.

STEP 4: Mix in the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients with a spatula. Make sure not to over mix by stopping the mxing once all the flour is gone.

STEP 5: Pre-scoop the cookie dough balls and refridgerate or freeze the cookie dough for at least 30 minutes.

STEP 6: After refridgerating, roll the cookie dough balls in cinnamon sugar made with 1/4 cup white sugar and 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

STEP 7: Finally bake at 350 degrees farinheit for 10-15 minutes. Let the cookies cool for at least 5 minutes then enjoy!
