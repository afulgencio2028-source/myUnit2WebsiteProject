Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of brown butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Kimberly Vardeman - Wikimedia Commons - Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies

Instructions

STEP 1: Brown 3/4 cup of butter. Then, let it cool to room temperature.

STEP 2: In a large bowl, mix together brown butter, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1/4 cup of white sugar until it forms almost like a paste that pulls away from the sides of the bowl.

STEP 3: Then, add in 1 room temperature egg yolk, and 1 whole room temperature egg. Add in the 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

STEP 4: In a SEPARATE bowl, whisk together all the dry ingredients - 1 3/4 cups of flour, 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt.

STEP 5: Mix in the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients with a spatula. Add in the chocolate chips. Make sure not to over mix by stopping the mxing once all the flour is gone.

STEP 6: Pre-scoop the cookie dough balls and refridgerate or freeze the cookie dough for at least 30 minutes. Finally bake at 350 degrees farinheit for 10-15 minutes. Let the cookies cool for at least 5 minutes then enjoy!
